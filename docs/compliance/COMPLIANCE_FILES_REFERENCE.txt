KEY IMPLEMENTATION POINTS
================================================================================

Consent Lifecycle (STORY-COMP-06 & 07):
  Domain: InpdpConsent aggregate with revoke() and is_valid()
  Service: InpdpConsentService with grant/revoke/dashboard
  Ports: IInpdpConsentRepository
  DTOs: GrantConsentRequest, RevokeConsentRequest, ConsentResponse, ConsentDashboardResponse

DPIA Workflow (STORY-COMP-08A):
  Domain: Dpia aggregate with state machine (Draft->UnderReview->Approved/Rejected)
  Service: DpiaService with create/submit/approve/reject methods
  Ports: IDpiaRepository
  DTOs: CreateDpiaRequest, UpdateDpiaStatusRequest, DpiaResponse

Breach Notification with 72h Compliance (STORY-COMP-08B):
  Domain: BreachNotification aggregate with 72h deadline validation
  Service: BreachNotificationService with notify_authority() checking deadline
  Ports: IBreachNotificationRepository with count_pending_authority_notification()
  DTOs: ReportBreachRequest, NotifyAuthorityRequest, BreachNotificationResponse

Data Portability & Erasure with Retention (STORY-COMP-09):
  Domain Models: DataPortabilityRequest, ErasureRequest
  Services: DataPortabilityService, ErasureService with check_erasure_eligibility()
  Ports: IDataPortabilityRepository, IErasureRepository
  DTOs: RequestDataPortabilityRequest, RequestErasureRequest, ErasureEligibilityResponse
